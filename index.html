carriedandheld.net focuses on the work of Park McArthur while also seeking a model of stewardship beyond the individual artist.



At present carriedandheld.net works directly with those interested in presenting, contextualizing, and experiencing the work of Park McArthur. Please get in touch regarding exhibition inquiries or loans, conservation, permission re-printing images or text, sales, provenance, fact checking, questions pertaining to academic syllabi or dissertations, or another matter not listed here.

Thank you for your interest.
